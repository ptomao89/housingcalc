How expensive is it to buy a home NYC?

By Pete Tomao

Home prices in New York City have hit record highs and have nearly doubled over the past 10 years. The median sales price of a home in New York now stands at $800,000. Roughly 2/3 New Yorker's rent and homeownership is increasingly out of reach for all but the wealthiest residents.

The pandemic super charged an already acute housing crisis, demand for space surged in a work-from-home economy. Space cramped urbanites went on the search for more spacious city lodgings. The most popular destination for Manhattan homebuyers: Brooklyn. Home prices in New York climbed 12.1% last year and rents have now exceeded their pre-pandemic highs. Take a look at where you can get the most bang for your buck in the city that never sleeps.
